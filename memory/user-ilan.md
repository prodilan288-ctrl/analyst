## Real KPI
Qualified DMs from serious artists and connectors. NOT follower count. NOT views.

## ICP signals (order matters)
1. Saves — artist bookmarking for later = serious interest
2. Watch time — content held attention = message landed
3. Comments with artist intent (collab mentions, genre-specific)
4. Profile visits from a reel

## What converts artists
- Placement proof content (MOFU) — the real moat
- Collabs with named producers/artists in captions
- Genre-specific CTAs that filter buyers (NOT "comment if you feel this" — that attracts broke beat-hunters; better CTAs direct to DM or ask about project/budget)
- Story time (Slovakia → US arc builds rare trust)
- Educational content aimed at artists (not producers)

## What does NOT convert
- Raw view counts (wrong audience watches)
- Generic motivational content (grows followers, not clients)
- Producer culture content (attracts producers, not artists)
- Cookups alone (shallow trust, not proof)
